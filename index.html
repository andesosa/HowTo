Asado Negro Recipe

Introduction

Among many Venezuelan families it is accustomed for the whole family to reunite during a weekday to have lunch. Most of the times the main entree for this lunch is the asado negro. the asado negro is one of Venezuela’s most famous and delicious customary foods. If you directly translate asado negro to english it would be black roast. It is called this way because of the black color of the sauce and the meat.It is common for each family to have their own unique and special asado negro recipe, so today I’m gonna share my own family’s recipe.

Recipe

Ingredients

Name Amount
Eye of Round Cut 3 pounds
Olive Oil 1/3 of a cup
Garlic 3 pieces
Water Until it fills the pan
Cayennes 1/2 of it
Tomatoes 1 whole
Onions 1 whole
Sweet Chili Peppers 3 pieces
Salt and Pepper As much as you want
Malta Beverage 1/2 a cup

First of all you should buy a eye of round cut and put it into a frying pan. The second step is to add some olive oil and garlic. Let it rest until the oil gains a gold and black color (this will help give the eye of round cut some flavor). Afterwards you’ll need to pour some water into the pan so that it covers the meat. Let it rest for a while and meanwhile you’ll need to get some cayennes, tomatoes, onion, and sweet chili peppers. Cut all these ingredients and put them into a blender (also add a little bit of water).

After blending the ingredients, add them to the pan (also add some salt and pepper to the mix). Let it fry for, maximum, an hour. Afterwards take the meat out of the pan and cut it into pieces. Put the meat strips back in the frying pan and add half a can of the beverage malta. Wait until the meat is tender and then it is ready to eat. Yet you are not finished, the asado negro is a food that needs to be eaten with various side dishes.

Side Dishes

  • Vegetables
  • Mashed Potatoes
  • Plantain
  • White Rice
  • Salad

There are various ways of accompanying this meal, the most popular ones are white rice and plantain. You can also accompany it with veggies,mashed potatoes, a or some salad. Also if you feel that you want to add or take away something to the recipe do it. Many families have different recipes, for example some use oranges and others use wine.
